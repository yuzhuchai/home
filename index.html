Zoey Blue

2022 - current

Zoey Blue is the alter ego and virtual cam girl of the artist Yuzhu Chai. @blue_zoey is a series of solo videos performed by the virtual avatar and uploaded to Only Fans. Her videos consist of solo performances while talking directly to the camera. Through Zoey’s narration, viewers follow her on a journey of self-examination and reflection to understand the artist's own sexual fetishes, desires, and taboos. The first debut video titled The Internet Was My Sex Education deals with the topic of sex education or the lack of sex education in China, as well as the impact of technology on intimacy, identities, and womanhood.

speak, pen

2019 - current

Texts have long been used as a medium of conveying both meaning and visual aesthetes. While some artists like Jenny Holzer and Barbara Kruger utilizes texts to get out powerful messages in their artwork, others like Ed Ruscha who’s painting often depicts texts as still life objects also emphasize the shapes and compositions of the words themselves. In concrete poetry, meaning is conveyed by graphic patterns of the texts, and rhythm is experienced through the composition. And for digital art, although unseen, every work is power by pieces of text – code.
Speak, Pen is a web-based drawing instrument programmed in JavaScript. It replaces the traditional paintbrush with text inputs. Users are able to create interactive drawings with text on the canvas. Created during a radical tool workshop, the work explores the materiality of text and the ways in which users experiment with text using the instrument beyond its semantic functions.

Flight Simulation

2022

Different types of flight simulator programs have been around since the 1920s. From training to entertainment purposes, those simulators aim to replicate how it feels like to operate a piece of technology that achieves human flying dreams. Unlike those traditional flight simulators, this work proposes an inflight simulation that is familiar to most human experiences when flying. As passengers, we are completely passive, we experience a loss of agency. Time becomes decompressed, oxygen is not natural, and we are untethered from a place and time. Flight Simulation is an interactive prose, where you click the linked text to get to the next paragraph. And it is the best experience with no rush.

poemHead

2020 - current

When I first came across a poetry website in search of a beloved poem, I was shocked to notice the attached voice reading of the poem is computer generated. It seems to be such an absurd and ridiculous way of portraying the art of poetry. I made the first video for Poemhead.com that day. Taking the absurdity to an extreme, PoemHead.com does not exist as a critique to the reading of poem, but a platform of a strange collection of poetries. It exists as another poetry website, where you will not come across poems but a new synthesis of anticipation and disorientation. Poems are now merely a medium. Perhaps, after lunch at work today, you opened up Poemhead.com, then it's not meant to be taken seriously. Open up a poem and enjoy.

ABOUT

Yuzhu Chai is an artist, creative technologist, and educator. Her work spans visual art, computation, and storytelling. She uses creative coding and game engines to create playful and poignant interactive stories inspired by the artist’s own experience. Growing up in a generation that experienced both floppy disk and immersive VR, technology has shaped the culture she grew up in, the daily experience, as well as the way people interact with each other. Her text-based projects examine how the player mechanism and the narrative can engage within an interactive work. Her work Speak, Pen is a drawing tool reminiscing the classic MS paint, where the players are able to create drawings on a canvas using a text-based paintbrush. Players are given the opportunity to create narrative with imagery, imagery from text, or both, and it is up to the player how they would use the tool to generate such work. Currently, she dedicates her studio practice to designing game-like projects that allow the player to engage with the theme meaningfully through the gameplay and interaction, turning the video game format into a unique and powerful art form and medium of storytelling.

She is an active member of the creative coding community, she has developed multiple workshops and online courses including the first official creative coding course on Bilibili. Her paper was published at the 2020 International Conference on Interactive Digital Storytelling. She also attended the 9th conference on Computation, Communication, Aesthetics & X as well as the ELO Conference and Festival: Platform (Post?) Pandemic in 2021.
